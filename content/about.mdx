I work in C++ on Unreal Engine, mostly on gameplay systems, netcode, and
reusable tools.

Currently at the indie studio Legion: We Are Many, building the Mirage Game
Framework, a modular server-authoritative base for action RPGs. Before that, I
shipped Evanescent as lead programmer.

## How I work

I care about code that lasts. Clean architecture, composition over inheritance,
and data-oriented design keep systems decoupled and easy to extend.

- **Architecture first.** Modular, data-driven systems over hardcoded one-offs.
- **Multiplayer from the start.** Server authority and replication by design,
  not bolted on later.
- **Tools for the team.** Reusable systems that keep other programmers unblocked.

## Beyond programming

I also work across game design, UI/UX, and a bit of 3D. Happy to talk Unreal,
replication, or framework design.

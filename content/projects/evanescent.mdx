## Overview

A single-player action-platformer in Unreal Engine 5. You move from forest to
caves, unlocking abilities along the way, in one continuous flow of exploration,
platforming, and combat.

Built by a team of five over four months, released on itch.io. I was Lead Game
Programmer.

## Combat System

A four-directional dash cancels any action instantly, so you're never locked in
an animation; in the air it's limited to one use before landing. A double jump
sets up plunging attacks that deal area damage on impact. The sword has light
and heavy attacks, plus a charged **Ultimate** (electrified blade) that briefly
slows time for aiming, then fires three energy slashes before a short cooldown.

## Grappling System

A grapple for both traversal and combat. At anchor points you launch while
keeping momentum, so movement chains naturally. In combat it's for repositioning,
dodging, and gaining height for plunges, without trivializing encounters.

Project Overview

Liminal Squad is a cooperative horror game where you play as a member of an elite intervention team. In a top-secret facility, scientists have opened a portal to the liminal spaces: eerie, mysterious places where the laws of physics no longer apply. Your mission: retrieve the cherub and escape the creatures that lurk within. Together with your squad, you'll have to navigate these haunting realms using your parkour skills. Cooperation is your only hope for survival... so choose your squad wisely — or be ready to serve as bait!

My Contribution

As the sole programmer on Liminal Squad, I’m responsible for the entire codebase, from gameplay mechanics to UI/UX, multiplayer systems, and developer tools. The game is built entirely in C++, with a strong focus on network replication and data persistence to ensure a smooth and immersive co-op experience.

Key areas I handle include :

Gameplay Programming : Core mechanics, movement systems (including parkour), creature AI, and mission logic.

Multiplayer Systems : Full replication setup, session management, and seamless co-op integration.

UI/UX : Designing and implementing a clear immersive interface, with a focus on modularity and dynamic feedback.

Developer Tools : Custom utilities for level designers and other team members to prototype and iterate quickly.

Project Architecture : Organizing the project structure, maintaining code consistency, and ensuring scalability.

Game Design Support : Collaborating on features, mechanics, and player experience to keep the gameplay coherent and engaging.

Technical Feasibility : Assessing the viability of proposed features and guiding design decisions based on performance and implementation constraints.

Visual Communication : Occasionally creating cinematic content and in-engine sequences to support the game's marketing and communication.

Pitch

Liminal Squad is a cooperative horror game where you play as a member of an elite response team. In a top-secret facility, scientists have opened a portal to the liminal spaces : mysterious and unsettling places where the laws of physics no longer apply.

Your mission : retrieve the cherub and escape the lurking horrors.

You and your squad must navigate these eerie, shifting environments using parkour skills and teamwork. Cooperation is your only hope of survivalâ€¦ so choose your squad wisely or be prepared to become the bait.
